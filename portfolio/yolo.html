1. Description


The site is a social network for active people who want to feel adrenaline anywhere in the world, are inspired by new ideas and just communicate. The site allows you to find the best events, no matter where the user is, no matter how fussy the idea would be. And if there are no ideas, the site will help to find the most interesting events nearby. The site helps to select friends in accordance with users interests.

The task of the project is to develop a mobile application of the IOS platform for the Roksolana hotel, which will simplify the process of check in and check out using an electronic key.


Object description:

Hotel Roksolana consists of six buildings and includes about 250 different types of rooms.

Problem:

Hotel complex has large number of rooms, the process of check in and check out is problematic.

About App:

The application "Hotel Roksolana" allows you to manage your reservations, pay for rooms and access to it any time without receptionist assistance or remote check-in, guest notifications and more. The application displays all the possible operations that are in the hotel. It is possible to view the plan of the hotel and plan of the specific room.

Search:

The search form and the output of the result is organized very simply, the use will be understandable for any user. A hotel site organizes its rooms into different types. Users can choose the number of adults, children and rooms on the search form and get the list of available rooms for definite dates.







Digital key


After paying for accommodation and providing identity documents, you can use the application to get the key to your room. This will provide easier access to the reserved rooms, anywhere at anytime. It’s keyless, cardless, and completely reshaping the standard for guest service.

• No need to stay in line
• Book a stay anywhere at anytime.


Features:

We created this application with additional features, such as the ability to preview the floor plan and a plan of the room in advance. A large number of filters and sorting makes it a little easier to organize a booking and immediately pay for it.



Main screens:

The presentation does not contain all possible scenarios. Only basic, to perform the main functions.

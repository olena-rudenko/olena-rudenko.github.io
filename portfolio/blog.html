Blogging app
Application is perfect for shorter posts with lots of visual content. The app is ideal for those who want to share photos and stories about their travels quickly anywhere to all social networks in one click.


Description:

This app gives bloggers complete access to editing and managing content in different social networks. You can create and edit posts, add photos, or just text. Keep track of comments and reply to readers in all accounts.



The Goal:

The main goal of this app is create and publish great content from anywhere. If you have many blogs, you’ll have easy access to all of them and be able to check on everything at once. Create, edit, and publish content, including images and videos. Receive notifications when someone comments and then edit and respond from within the app.

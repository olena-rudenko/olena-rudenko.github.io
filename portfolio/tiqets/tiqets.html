CASE STUDY:

Showing tickets according to availability Currently in the Tiqets app's city screen we show all products attached to the selected city. There is no distinction made based on availability of the tickets. It may be that some tickets are available for today, but some are sold out or unavailable for some other reason. We know that many of our customers buy their tickets last-minute, even on the same day; as a tourist it could be useful to be able to somehow differentiate between tickets which are available right now vs those not available.
